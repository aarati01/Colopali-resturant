About Us

Let's know who we are

Welcome to Colopalli Resort, where the magic of two diverse worlds converges to create an unforgettable experience. Founded by two adventurous spirits, Kabita from the enchanting hills of Nepal and Romina from the vibrant landscapes of Colombia, our resort is a celebration of cultural fusion and lifelong friendship. In the heart of the city, where diverse cultures meet and mingle, Colopalli Resort stands as a testament to the rich tapestry of international flavors. The story begins with two passionate chefs, Kabita and Romina, who, despite coming from opposite ends of the globe, found a common love for food and a shared dream of creating a dining experience that transcends borders. Kabita and Romina, the dynamic duo behind Global Palate Bistro, didn't just want to create a restaurant; they aimed to build a cultural bridge between Nepal and Colombia. To achieve this, they traveled extensively, immersing themselves in the culinary traditions and lifestyles of both countries.
